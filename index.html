About the Self-Storage Awards

Learn more about our mission and how we recognize excellence in the self-storage industry.

The Self-Storage Awards were established in 2020 to celebrate the outstanding achievements of self-storage facilities across the nation. Our goal is to provide a platform where facilities are recognized by the community for their dedication to excellence in service, innovation, and security.

We believe that recognizing and rewarding outstanding facilities is critical for fostering healthy competition and overall growth in the self-storage industry. Through these awards, we aim to encourage the highest standards of service and innovation.

Winners receive medals, digital badges to display on their websites, and significant visibility within the industry and among their customers. Submitting your facility is a simple way to gain recognition.

The voting is open to the public, which helps ensure fair representation and a transparent evaluation process. We invite you to explore the facilities, vote for your favorites, and submit your own facilities for consideration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to your most common questions about the awards.

How often can I vote for a facility?

You can vote once per facility per day. This rule helps ensure fair representation.

What is the submission fee?

The submission fee is $14.99 per facility. This fee covers the administration costs associated with the competition.

What are the benefits of winning an award?

Winners receive physical medals, digital badges for their website, and are promoted on our platform to increase their exposure.

When does the voting period end?

Voting ends on December 31st, 2025. See our countdown clock on the homepage for the remaining time.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team via the contact us form, email us directly or give us a call .
